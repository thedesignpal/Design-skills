\# GoodSama — Brand Guidelines

\*Visual identity reference for the app, landing page, and all design assets.\*

\---

\#\# Moodboard Inspiration

\*\*Source:\*\* Pinterest editorial poster collection  
\*\*Style direction:\*\* Bold typographic posters with high-contrast orange, warm neutrals, and strong editorial energy. Functional objects (traffic lights, cars, keyboards) used as metaphors. Text-as-image aesthetic — type carries the emotion.

\---

\#\# Brand Personality

\*\*What GoodSama feels like:\*\*  
\- Urgent but warm — this matters, and someone nearby cares  
\- Direct — no fluff, no corporate softness  
\- Community-rooted — street-level, human, real  
\- Action-oriented — not awareness, \*response\*

\*\*What GoodSama does not feel like:\*\*  
\- A charity donation platform (sad, guilt-driven)  
\- A government app (cold, bureaucratic)  
\- A social media feed (noisy, dopamine-driven)

\#\# Typography

\#\#\# Type Scale

| Role | Style | Size | Weight |  
|------|-------|------|--------|  
| Display / Hero | DM Serif Display | 48–64px | Regular |  
| Screen Title | DM Serif Display | 26–32px | Regular |  
| Section Label | DM Sans | 13px | 600, uppercase, tracked |  
| Body | DM Sans | 14–16px | 400 |  
| Input Text | DM Sans | 15–18px | 400 |  
| Hint / Caption | DM Sans | 11–12px | 400 |

\#\#\# Type Rules  
\- Headlines: sentence case only. Never full caps on multi-word headings.  
\- Section labels (e.g. "YOUR NUMBER", "STEP 1 OF 3"): uppercase \+ letter-spacing: 1px  
\- Serif for emotion (brand name, screen welcome). Sans for function (forms, labels, buttons).  
\- Letter-spacing on phone number inputs: 2px — creates breathing room for digits

\---

\#\# Logo & Mark

\- \*\*Wordmark:\*\* GoodSama — set in DM Serif Display  
\- \*\*Symbol:\*\* 🐾 paw print — used as app icon, section marker, and emotional anchor  
\- \*\*Tagline:\*\* \*Making animal rescue faster, safer, and community-driven.\*  
\- \*\*Minimum size:\*\* Wordmark no smaller than 20px rendered size

\---

\#\# Iconography

\- Style: outline only, no fills  
\- Weight: consistent 1.5–2px stroke  
\- Preferred library: Tabler Icons (outline set)  
\- Common icons in use: location pin, lock, arrow-right, phone, check, bell

\---

\#\# Illustration & Photography Style

Inspired directly by the moodboard:  
\- \*\*Bold type-as-image:\*\* Large, condensed sans-serif words over texture or a strong photo crop  
\- \*\*Object metaphors:\*\* Real objects (paws, collars, tags, phones) shot flat or close-cropped  
\- \*\*Split compositions:\*\* Half colour block / half photo, or large type bleeding off the edge  
\- \*\*Texture:\*\* Light canvas/grain texture on orange or cream surfaces — not smooth digital flat  
\- \*\*No stock-photo animals looking sad\*\* — this is about action, not guilt

\#\# Voice & Tone

| Situation | Tone | Example |  
|-----------|------|---------|  
| Welcome / onboarding | Warm, purposeful | \*"Join a network of animal welfare helpers near you."\* |  
| Notification | Direct, specific | \*"There's an injured dog 1.2km from you. Can you help in the next hour?"\* |  
| Privacy note | Reassuring, brief | \*"Your number is never shared with other users."\* |  
| Empty state | Encouraging | \*"No reports near you right now. You're all set to help when it matters."\* |  
| Error | Clear, not alarming | \*"Enter a 10-digit number to continue."\* |

\#\#\# Voice Rules  
\- No exclamation marks in functional copy  
\- No guilt language ("animals are suffering…")  
\- No vague CTAs ("Click here", "Learn more") — every button should say what it does  
\- Address the user directly: "You", "Your", not "Users" or "Members"

\---

\#\# What to Avoid

\- Purple/blue gradients as primary palette (old \`index.html\` — this is being replaced)  
\- Rounded pill buttons (too soft for this brand)  
\- Drop shadows and glow effects  
\- Cute or infantilising animal illustration  
\- Charity-campaign visual language
